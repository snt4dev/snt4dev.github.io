The Project

SnT4Dev: Applied Research in ICT in West Africa, by Setting up a Research and Technology Organisation-like Institution with a Regional Outreach

The initiative, titled “SnT4Dev”, aims to help addressing the insufficient amount of capable scientists for conducting research and teaching within local universities. In the short term, it offers funding for hiring the ones trained by ongoing initiatives such as LuxWays; and in the medium term it aims at disseminating the culture of research in the private sector, building private sector capacities as well as supporting the local governments’ policy on fostering higher education.
